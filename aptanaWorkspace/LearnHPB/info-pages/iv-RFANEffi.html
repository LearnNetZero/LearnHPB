RETURN FAN MOTOR OVERALL EFFICIENCY

Associated System: Fan

The Return Fan overall efficiency compares the output horsepower of the fan to the horsepower demand. In essence, this value describes the overall effectiveness of the fan.

The value of this variable can be adjusted during a simulation run-time.

Variable Descriptions

  • 0.5
  • 0.1
  • 1
