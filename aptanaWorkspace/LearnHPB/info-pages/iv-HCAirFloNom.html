HEATING COIL DESIGN AIR FLOW RATE

Associated System: Heating / Cooling Coils

This parameter sets the design air flow rate capacity. This value signifies the maximum air flow rate under design conditions.

The value of this variable can not be adjusted during simulation run-time. It may be adjusted before or after a simulation.
