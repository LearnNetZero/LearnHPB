HOT WATER PUMP OVERALL EFFICIENCY

Associated System: Boiler

The Hot Water Pump overall efficiency compares the output horsepower of the pump to the horsepower demand. In essence, this value describes the overall effectiveness of the chilled water pumps.

Lowering the efficiency will force the chilled water pumps to increase the power demand of the pump motor as it tries to maintain the pressure and water flow. Raising this value will reduce the power demand an increase the central plant efficiency.

The value of this variable can not be adjusted during simulation run-time. It may be adjusted before or after a simulation.
