HVAC SYSTEM OPERATION START TIME

Associated System: Simulation

This parameter assigns a regular time for the HVAC System to start operating.The start-time is determined by the typical start of occupancy of a building. Typically the start time anticipates the heating and cooling demands by an appropriate offset (i.e. an hour or two before a building's tenants will arrive.)

The operation start-time will determine if the HVAC system is efficiently addressing the needs of the occupants. If the start-time is too late the building may not be adequately heated before occupants arrive. On the other hand, excessively early settings will impact the total energy consumption with unnecessary operation.

The value of this variable can not be adjusted during simulation run-time. It may be adjusted before or after a simulation.
