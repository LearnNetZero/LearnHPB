VAV-BOXES

Variable Air Volume (VAV) Boxes, provide air directly into the zones. The air is delivered to the boxes from HVAC ducts (typically located in the space the zone's ceiling). These boxes are equipped with dampers which open an close depending on the need for conditioned air. VAV boxes may also be equipped with a hot water coil in order to "re-heat" the air when necessary in order to maintain comfortable levels within the zone.
