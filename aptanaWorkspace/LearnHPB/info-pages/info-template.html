Heating Coil Design Heating Capacity

Associated System: Heating Coil

INSERT IMAGE [heating-coil-design-heating-capacity.png]
This parameter sets the design heating capacity of the heating coil located in the air handler on the roof. (See also the design heating capacity of the heating coil located in the VAV box of each zone.)
Adjusting this variable will change the heating capacity of the heating coil used.

The value of this variable can not be adjusted during simulation run-time. It may be adjusted before or after a simulation.
