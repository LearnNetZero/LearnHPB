ZONE 4 VAV MIN VOLUME FLOW RATE RATIO

Associated System: VAV Boxes

This value represents the minimum airflow rate that the variable air volume box can allow.

Adjusting this value alter the total airflow into the zone. Increasing the maximum airflow will increase the speed of the fan motor as it attempts to maintain the zone conditions based on the current set point. A decrease will reduce the airflow and fan power demand.

The value of this variable can be adjusted during a simulation run-time.
