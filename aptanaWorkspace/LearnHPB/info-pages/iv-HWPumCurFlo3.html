HOT WATER PUMP VOLUME FLOW RATE AT THIRD OPERATING POINT

Associated System: Boiler

This parameter is a single point in the heating hot water pump's system curve which defines the total heating hot water flow at some given index, typically the total system head pressure. As the ambient conditions outside of a building decrease, a building's HVAC system will require more heating from heating hot water delivered to the various heating coils as those of an air handling unit.

An increase in the value will increase the demand for heating hot water flow at the third operating point

The value of this variable can not be adjusted during simulation run-time. It may be adjusted before or after a simulation.

Variable Descriptions

  • 0.00048
  • 0
  • 0.001
  • GPM
  • m3/s
