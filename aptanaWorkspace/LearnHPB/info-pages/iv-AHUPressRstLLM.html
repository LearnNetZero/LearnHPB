DUCT STATIC PRESSURE RESET LOW LIMIT

Associated System: HVAC Distribution System

The low limit is the minimum allowable static pressure within a reset strategy. Typically this is set point during low occupancy or demand.

The value of this variable can be adjusted during a simulation run-time.
