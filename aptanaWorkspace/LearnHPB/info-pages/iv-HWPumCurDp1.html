HOT WATER PUMP TOTAL PRESSURE AT FIRST OPERATING POINT

Associated System: Boiler

This parameter is a single point in the heating hot water pump's system curve which defines the system pressure at some given index, typically the pumps' total heating hot water flow. As the ambient conditions outside of a building descend in temperature, a building's HVAC system will require more heating in the form of heating hot water delivered to the various heatng coils, such as those of an air handling unit.

An increase in the value will increase the HVAC system's heating hot water pressure at the first operating point

The value of this variable can not be adjusted during simulation run-time. It may be adjusted before or after a simulation.
