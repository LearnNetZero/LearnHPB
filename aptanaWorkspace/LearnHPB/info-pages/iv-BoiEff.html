BOILER EFFICIENCY

Associated System: Boiler

This parameter sets the boiler efficiency. Boiler efficiency is a ratio of the Boilers Thermal output (the heating supplied to a system medium such as hot water or steam) to the boiler's fuel usage (the energy input.) Essentially this value determines how effectively a boiler converts fuel (ie natural gas or electricity) to useable heating energy (ie. hot water or steam).

Raising the boiler efficiency will reduce the fueldemand of the boiler over time. Alternatively, lowering this value will cause the boiler to increase fuel consumption.

The value of this variable can not be adjusted during simulation run-time. It may be adjusted before or after a simulation.
