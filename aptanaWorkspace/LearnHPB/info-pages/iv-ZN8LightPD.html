ZONE 8 (NORTH) MAXIMUM LIGHTING POWER DENSITY

Associated System: Zones

This parameter sets the maximum ligthing power allowed in the zone. The value is the ratio between the total ligthing power demand (kW) and a predetermined area, usually defined by type (in this case, a 'zone').

Changes to this parameter will directly affect the power consumption of the building. Indirectly, it will also increase the heat inside the building, there increasing the cooling demand and the HVAC system's power demand

The value of this variable can not be adjusted during simulation run-time. It may be adjusted before or after a simulation.
