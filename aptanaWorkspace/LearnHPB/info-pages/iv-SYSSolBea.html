DIRECT NORMAL (BEAM) SOLAR RADIATION

Associated System: Weather data

The direct normal (beam) solar radiaton parameter determines the amount of direct sunlight and, consequently, it's contribution to the heat load of the building. This component contributes to the building's cooling (or heating) demand and the response of the HVAC system. As the sun approaches the horizon, this amount of radiation will decrease.

Adjustments to this value will impact the response of the HVAC system. Decreasing this value will cause a reduction in the total heat load on the building.

The value of this variable can be adjusted during a simulation run-time.
