SUPPLY (RETURN) FAN SPEED CONTROL STRATEGY

Associated System: Fan

A Supply Fan Speed control Strategy is a control system algorithm that alters the supply fan speed based on the current cooling or heating demand of the building. Typically, the fan speed is adjusted in order to produce the correct amount of pressure inside of the supply air ducts that provide conditioned air to the spaces. The speed of the motor that powers the fan can be controlled by a Variable Frequency Drive (VFD) which directly adjusts the speed of the motor by varying the power supplied.

The value of this variable can be adjusted during a simulation run-time.
