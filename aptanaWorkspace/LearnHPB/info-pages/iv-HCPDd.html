HEATING COIL DESIGN AIR SIDE PRESSURE DROP

Associated System: Heating / Cooling Coils

The heating coil design air side pressure drop is the difference in pressure across the heating fan coils in and air handler during design conditions. Coils inserted into the air stream of an HVAC unit will by necessity cause a certain amount of the loss that the fans of the system must overcome in order to provide that correct amount of air for that system.

Increasing this amount will produce an additional pressure drop that the fan must overcome. As a result the fan must increase the air flow being provided into the system.

The value of this variable can not be adjusted during simulation run-time. It may be adjusted before or after a simulation.
