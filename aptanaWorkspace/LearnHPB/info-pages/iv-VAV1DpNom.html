VAV1 AIR SIDE PRESSURE DROP AT MAX COOLING

Associated System: VAV Boxes

This input sets the air-side pressure drop across the VAV box during maximum (design) cooling.

The value of this variable can not be adjusted during simulation run-time. It may be adjusted before or after a simulation.
