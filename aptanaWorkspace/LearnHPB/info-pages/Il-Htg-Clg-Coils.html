HTG-CLG-COILS

Air Handing Units are equipped with chilled water coils and possibly hot water coils in order to condition the air sent to the building zones. The air is conditioned via a heat exchange with water from the central plant. This exhange happens by air traveling through the coils which is then cooled or heated by the coils. As a consequence of the exchange, the water leaving coils is heated (chilled water coils) or cooled (hot water coils).
