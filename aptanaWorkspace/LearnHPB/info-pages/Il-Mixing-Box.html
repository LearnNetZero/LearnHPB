MIXING-BOX

A mixing box is a component of an air handling unit (AHU) that provides air to the chilled and hot water coils. Inside the mixing box, outdoor air is mixed with hot air returning from the building before being sent to the coils. If the AHU is equipped with an economizer, the amount of outdoor air introduced into the AHU will be allowed to vary, tempering the return air with cool outside air and reducing the need for cooling at the chilled water coils.
