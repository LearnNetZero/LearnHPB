City name

Associated System: Geographical Location

Current location of the Building Model. This variable will determine the location's ambient conditions that are determined by typical weather parameters, sunlight exposure etc. and consequently the response of the various systems of the building.

Adjustments to this variable will impact the amount of heating and cooling required in order to maintain comfortable conditions within the building. In addition, the location may determine the type of HVAC equipment required.

This variable must be set prior to simulation run-time.
