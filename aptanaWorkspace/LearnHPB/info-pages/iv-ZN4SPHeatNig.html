ZONE 4 (SOUTHWEST) HEATING SET POINT NIGHT SETBACK

Associated System: Zones

A heating set point nighttime setback is a strategy that adjusts the zone temperature heating set point according to a predetermined schedule. The set point setback strategy may be controlled by a building's HVAC Control System (ie. Energy Management System) or directly at a programmable schedule. Schedules are usually created for Weekdays, Weekends and evenings.

A heating nighttime setback stratgegy relaxes the heating demand during unoccupied hours. The intention of the setback is to eliminate the operation of an HVAC system and, as a result, reduce energy costs.

The value of this variable can be adjusted during a simulation run-time.
