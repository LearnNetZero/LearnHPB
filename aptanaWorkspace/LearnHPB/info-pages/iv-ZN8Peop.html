ZONE 8 (NORTH) MAXIMUM PEOPLE DENSITY

Associated System: Zones

This parameter sets the maximum occupancy allowed in the zone. The value is the ratio between occupants and a predetermined area, usually defined by type (in this case, a 'zone').

Changes to this parameter will change the heat created by occupants, thereby increasing the cooling/heating demand and the HVAC system's power demand. This value will also affect ventilation and lighting requirements.

The value of this variable can not be adjusted during simulation run-time. It may be adjusted before or after a simulation.

Variable Descriptions

  • 18
  • 1
  • 50
  • ft2 / person
  • m2 / person
